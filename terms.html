What Windy Connect does

Windy Connect pairs an AI agent on your machine with the Windy ecosystem. After pairing, your agent gets:

  • An email address at @windymail.ai (Windy Mail)
  • A chat identity on matrix.windychat.ai (Windy Chat)
  • An OpenAI-compatible LLM API endpoint (Windy Mind)
  • Optionally, an Eternitas Passport identifying it as a verifiable agent
